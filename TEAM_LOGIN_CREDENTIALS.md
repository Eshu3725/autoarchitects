## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### **Problem: "Invalid email or password"**

**Solutions:**
1. ✅ Check your email is typed correctly (no spaces)
2. ✅ Check your USN is typed correctly (case-sensitive)
3. ✅ Make sure you're using the exact USN from the table
4. ✅ Try copying and pasting your credentials

**Example:**
- ❌ Wrong: `1si22me021` (lowercase)
- ✅ Correct: `1SI22ME021` (uppercase)

### **Problem: "Can't access the website"**

**Solutions:**
1. ✅ Make sure the dev server is running
2. ✅ Check the URL is correct
3. ✅ Try refreshing the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
4. ✅ Clear browser cache and try again

### **Problem: "Redirected to wrong dashboard"**

**Solutions:**
1. ✅ Logout and login again
2. ✅ Clear browser cookies
3. ✅ Contact admin to verify your role
